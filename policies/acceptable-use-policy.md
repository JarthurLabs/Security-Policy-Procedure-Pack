# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose
Define acceptable use of company systems, applications, accounts, and data.

## Key Requirements
- Use company systems only for approved business purposes.
- Do not share passwords or MFA codes.
- Lock workstations when unattended.
- Report suspicious emails, pop-ups, or account activity.
- Do not install unauthorized software.

## Why This Matters
Acceptable use reduces preventable security incidents caused by unsafe user behavior.
