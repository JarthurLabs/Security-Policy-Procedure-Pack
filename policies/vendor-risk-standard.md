# Vendor Risk Standard

## Owner

Business Owner

## Purpose

Define how new vendors are reviewed before approval.

## Vendor Review Triggers

A vendor review is required when a vendor will:

- Store customer data
- Process billing or financial data
- Access internal systems
- Integrate with business-critical SaaS platforms
- Handle employee data
- Provide security-sensitive services

## Minimum Review Requirements

- Business owner identified
- Data type documented
- MFA/SSO support reviewed
- Incident notification language reviewed
- Data deletion/export reviewed
- Subprocessors reviewed where available
- Risk score documented
- Approval decision recorded

## Approval Outcomes

- Approved
- Conditional approval
- Rejected
- More information needed
